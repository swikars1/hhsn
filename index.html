KNOW ABOUT US

Helping Heart Society Nepal, established in 2009 A.D is a non-profitable organization working in Nepal since then with a motive of “Working for the welfare of needy people”. Its sectors of work are - to fight against human trafficking, to work for youth and women empowerment, scholarship programs for kids, providing for the marginalized groups and spreading awareness through educational programs among them.

HSSN has been particularly working to fight against Human trafficking with a mission to save young Nepali Daughters and children from being a victim and a prey to the evil sex industry.

OUR WORK SECTOR

A. Anti-human trafficking

HSSN works for the rescuing and prevention of Nepali girls from being trafficked across India-Nepal open border, golf states and many more countries.

  • HOW WE WORK

Border surveillance, information sharing, counseling and rescue

Our booths in different bus stations that conducts the road way transportation from Nepal to India are taken in charge with well-equipped and trained counselors who are always strictly focused on surveillance and monitoring of the situations around. Any suspicious activities and suspects observed they are dedicated to their work for counseling the girls after inquiry and different other processes of investigation. They are also working for information sharing and the booths are help desks for people needing help.

Emergency shelter, safe homes and training center

The girls rescued and probable victims with seriously devastating situations after their case observation are brought to safety with the purpose of providing them with an environment where they are provided with psychological, mental and spiritual counseling. They are given the opportunity for different vocational training.

Skill development and Income generating programs

ASSN has been working to provide the skillful training to the rescued individuals as well as to the women belonging to the marginalized and indigent groups. Trainings like sewing and boutique, beautician training and fast food making training are some of them that ASSN has been providing so that after the completion the trainee would be able to self-sustain and be able to earn their own living to be self- dependent.

Rehabilitation

The psychological situation, mental state, lack of skills and inability to be socially adjustable to the society is a big obstacle for the victims to be rehabilitated in the society after all they have been through. ASSN has been making efforts to psychologically, financially and socially support them and make them capable of rehabilitating in society like every other human being.

Awareness, advocacy and lobbying

One of our major work has been publicly creating awareness among people about human trafficking. Through street dramas, help desks as booths in stations, brochure and public programs awareness focusing on cause, affects and its control and solution are performed periodically.

B. Child Sponsership

Millions of kids in Nepal are deprived of their basic needs like education, food, emotional and psychological care, health care and many more, the reason for which are various factors like being an orphan, weak family financial situation, a society unaware of importance of education, patriarchal mindset that objects the education of girls, and unavailability of basic needs due to unsuitable geographical and infrastructural development reasons.
